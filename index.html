Why Bluekid?

Bluekid is an online learning platform very simular to Blooket and Kahoot. It helps kids learn and be more engaged then listen to someone talk. Bluekid is designed to be easy to use and fun.


Fun

Us at Bluekid want students to have fun but secretly be learning at the same time. That is where our gamemodes and blues come into play. Blues are a fun way for children to customize their apperence. Gamemodes bring differnt fun for each time you play!

Learning

You choose what and how the students learn. You can find, create, and use sets to play games! If the students want to go even father, they can host their own games with their own sets!

Everyone can play!

Everyone, even yourself can play! Do you want to have fun with the students? Just make sure you turn on the setting!


Bluekid has a currency.

Bluekid has a currency for children to buy packs and receive new characters. The currency is not obtainable with real money. You earn this currency by playing games and participating.


Bluekid is managed by one person.

Yes. I made Bluekid myself with a little bit of help over at Firebase. If any servers do not work or are down, please let me handle the situation. After about 3:00 PM EST I can fix these issues. See more info

What are blues?

Blues are fun characters that love to play games! You can collect blues with buying packs. You can buy them when you sign up!
