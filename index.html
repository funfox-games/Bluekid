So what is this, "Bluekid" thing?

Inspired off of Blooket, Bluekid provides free, fun learning! Here are the essentaials:

Play Games

Either hosting or playing,
get to participate in serval fun different gamemodes!

Earn Awards

Get coins, buy packs!
Get fun blues, get other awesome treats!

Have Fun

Play user created kits,
and get awards!


Customization!

Bluekid gives the option to customize your character! You can also personalize your experince with the different settings.



How is it done?

It's not that hard! Follow the steps below to get a fast kickstart in Bluekid.


1. Create an account

Create an account to get into the fun! An account is not needed for joinning, but is recommended!

2. Choose / create a kit

Kits hold a bunch of settings and questions to play with!

3. Select a mode

There is several gamemodes to choose from. New modes are activly being created!

4. Host / Join

Join your teacher in a game or just have some fun!

5. See results

See the players avarage, see what was the most missed, etc.
